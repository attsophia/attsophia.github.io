Served aside
205x205mm
2016
Set of cards introducing a dozen traditional moroccan side meals. Echoing the instinctive aspect of the north african and middle eastern cuisine, and the intimacy that is to be engaged with each dish, the set ignores the usual quasi scientific approach to meal preparation and leaves room for deviation and experimentation.
