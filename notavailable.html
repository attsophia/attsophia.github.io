Not available festival
Declined identity on various mediums
2016
Fictional multidisciplinary festival that takes as its starting point the problematisation of the often encountered internet notification ‘‘content not available in your country’’: how are geographical boundaries embodied within a space that once was acclaimed precisely for its borderless aspects? How are states paradoxically excercising more and more their soverignty on the cyberspace? And how is that symptomatic of the current societal malaise? The n/a festival interogates the myth of the borderless world in the present context and aims to elevate the debate of the borders, of any nature, from a cultural and aesthetical approach, by operating as a forum and implementing a space for dialogue and expression, hosting a series of acts, live performances, art displays, film screenings and workshops.
