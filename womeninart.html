Women in art (school), or I swear next time someone tells me there are not much female artists anyway
594x841mm
2018
Series of posters part of a campaign held at the Royal Academy of Art aiming to point out the lack of visibility of female artists in art education – the poster is laminated and enables the audience to add content to one's liking.
