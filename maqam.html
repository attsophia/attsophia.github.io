Maqam.tv
Screening station
2019 (Ongoing)
Maqam is the migratory film screening station part of Please fill in the empty space, a piece weaving through performance, rhetoric and story-telling. Maqam explores alternative topologies for MENA+ (Middle-East and North-Africa) landscapes, bodies and tales and addresses the bizarre general state of amnesia segments of the region's audio-visual heritage have been subject to.
Explore Maqam online: instagram.com/maqam.tv or get in touch if you'd like to collaborate or host a screening session or two.
