Maqām.tv
Broadcasting channel
Ongoing
Maqam.tv is the virtual and physical migratory film screening station part of Please fill in the empty space, a piece weaving through performance, story-telling and academic lecturing. Maqam.tv is one contemporary adaptation of the traditional tv channel. It might stand somewhere between the latter and a theater stage, on wheels though. It is mobile and fluid, articulate and generous. It functions like an itinerant broadcasting channel. The term of broadcasting is in fact taken in its two senses here: for one thing, it’s a matter of communicating a given content. And for the other, it’s a matter of sowing seeds in a given area by hand. It’s a hand that collects, organizes, selects, amplifies, and it does not deny the subjectivity of its grip.
Maqam.tv builds up dramaturgies, and it does so by widening towards a plethora of knowledge, art and cultural practices to tell stories; sometimes to tell them for the first time, sometimes to re-tell them, sometimes to put them back into circulation. It does love agitation indeed; strives in the rearrangement of prefixes too. It gets its resilience from theories and practices of liberation, restoration and healing. And grew from the very urgency in addressing distribution matters locally, in decentralizing archiving and transmission processes, appropriating the North-African and South-West Asian broadcasting circuit at last; that’s for the dynamics to get shifted, for our thought to be retrieved.
