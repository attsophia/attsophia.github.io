Fear and loathing in the EU
Open website in a new window
2018
An encounter with the absurd, the risible and the bitter through a growing collection of individual fables. The web platform is for non-EU nationals to share their Kafkaesque bureaucratic experiences and the ciruclar and nonsensical logic in which they are forced to navigate.
